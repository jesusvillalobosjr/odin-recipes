Sushi Recipe

Ingredients

  • Sushi rice
  • Rice wine vinegar
  • Seaweed
  • Wasabi Paste
  • Smoked salmon
  • Cucumber
  • Avocado

Steps

  1. Soak and cook the rice.
  2. Mix in the vinegar and cool in an even layer on a plate.
  3. Smoke the salmon.
  4. Cut to prefferred style the cucumber and avocado.
  5. Join ingredients and roll sushi.
  6. Cut sushi into 8 pieces.
  7. Put wasabi paste on the side.
